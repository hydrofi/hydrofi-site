HydroFi (HYFI) — Whitepaper

v1.0 · 2025-09-07

Abstract

HydroFi (HYFI) is a water-inspired, energy-aware cryptocurrency built on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20). Our mission is to combine financial innovation with environmental responsibility, delivering a fast, affordable token with transparent mechanics, locked liquidity, and long-term founder vesting.

Vision & Rationale

Water sustains life through constant flow, clarity and renewability. HYFI brings these principles to crypto: efficient transactions, clean transparency and sustainable growth. We prioritise low resource usage, responsible token distribution and verifiable on-chain commitments.

Technology Overview

  • Chain / Standard: BNB Smart Chain • BEP-20
  • Token: HYFI (0xc28dF9EbAD0D8A1E8Ab4480F3C94277d182e42e9)
  • Total Supply: 100,000,000,000 HYFI (18 decimals)
  • Core Features: Anti-whale limits (maxTx 1%, maxWallet 2%); optional transaction cooldown; blacklist-freeze; owner utilities (burnable/ownable/rescueTokens).
  • Transparency: Contracts verifiable on BscScan; liquidity & founder allocations locked.

Environmental Focus

  • Lower energy footprint: BNB Smart Chain is significantly more energy-efficient than PoW chains, reducing per-tx emissions.
  • Support for green initiatives: Potential allocation for water purification, renewables and circular-economy pilots.
  • Radical transparency: Clear on-chain records, public addresses and time-locked schedules.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 100,000,000,000 HYFI
  • Liquidity: Locked via PinkLock — token lock, pair lock.
  • Founder Vesting: FounderLocker — 200M HYFI per partner every 30 days, 12-month cliff, 36-month duration.
  • Community / Treasury / Marketing: To be published with on-chain references.

Mechanics & Protections

  • Trading enablement after initial setup & liquidity.
  • Anti-whale tx and wallet limits.
  • Optional cooldown to mitigate bots.
  • Blacklist-freeze to improve governance certainty.
  • Third-party reviews & audits (to be published).
